Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that combines a rich and savory meat

sauce with perfectly cooked spaghetti. The sauce is made with ground beef, pancetta or

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

Ground Beef 100g Pancetta or Bacon

1 Onion, 1 Carrot, 1 Celery stalk, 2 cloves Garlic (minced)

1 tbsp Tomato Paste, beef stock, Spaghetti and Parmesan cheese for serving

Canned Crushed Tomatoes, Salt, Pepper, Dried Oregano and a pinch of sugar

Instructions

1.Fill a pot with water, add a little salt, and bring it to a boil.

2. Add your spaghetti and cook for 8–10 minutes

3. n a pan, heat some oil. Add chopped onions, garlic, and a little chopped pepper

4. Fry for 2–3 minutes until soft.

5. Add minced beef (or chicken/turkey) to the pan

6. Stir and cook until browned

7. Add seasoning, salt, and a little curry or thyme if you like.

8. Pour in blended or canned tomatoes / tomato paste

9. Stir well and let it simmer for 10–15 minutes until

10. the sauce thickens. You can add a little water if it’s too thick

11. Put the spaghetti on a plate and pour the meat sauce on top

12. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and serve hot.

Enjoy your meal!
